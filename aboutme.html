About Me

My name is Chiara Hodge, and I am a 17 year old Stage Manager from Manhattan, New York City. I attend LaGuardia Highschool of Music and Arts and the Performing Arts as a Technical Theater major. Past PSM Credits: The Seagull, Mr Lobenhofer's Senior Drama One Acts, Mr Davidson's Senior Drama One Acts [LaGuardia High School]; Past SM Credits: Our Town: Multilingual Edition; Gilbert and Sullivans The Gondoliers; Spring Semi-Annual Concert [LaGuardia Highschool]; ASM Credits: Fun Home [Nxt Generation Theatrics]; Ms Fries Senior Drama One Acts [LaGuardia High School). Outside of tech, I enjoy writing poetry and fiction, and spending time with my family, friends, and cats. CURRENT: ASM on Sunday in the Park with George [Nxt Generation Theatrics]
